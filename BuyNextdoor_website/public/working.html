Business/Pricing Model

For Campus corps

  • The pricing for campus corpsis(a link here) essentially a subscription type of model.
  • Once the campus corp chooses a subscription, whatever gross profit he/she generates while operating BuyNextDoor in his/her campus will belong to them.
  • For example if the revenue i.e cost of goods participating in the transaction (purchase/rent) for a particular month is INR 15000, and if the transaction fees is 10%, which totals to INR 1500, will be transferred to Campus corp's account.

For those want to make transactions on the mobile app

  • The pricing for this category is fee per transaction model and paid add-on benifits.
  • When a person makes a paid transaction, a percentage on total bill amount will be charged as a transaction fee.
  • On a succesful transaction, all the essential details of the seller will be sent on the buyer's dashboard.
Campus corps

Our Campus corp Program

What will be the role of a campus corp?

  • The entire operation of BuyNextDoor in a particular college/campus will be handled solely by a campus corp only.
  • The sole task of talking to customers, handling their queries, promoting the mantra of BuyNextDoor will be handled by him/her.
  • Ultimately be the face of BuyNextDoor in your college/campus, create a buzz around the brand and help us grow.

Who all can become a part of campus corp program?

  • We are looking for highly enthusiastic and corp students (No requirement of any prior business/startup knowledge) who are currently enrolled in any undergraduate program, who falls under our operational areas and who will help us establish the executional process.
  • Students who wish to aqcuire knowledge on how startups work, improve their management skills, work on their style of pitching, enhancing their decision making skills, knowing and implementing various marketing and promotional activities.

What will be given to you/rewards?

  • As described in the above section of our business model, the transaction fee of 10% per transaction model will be solely yours if you are our the monthly subscriber.
  • Top Campus corps will be featured on the website every week and top performers every month will be awarded incentives.
  • Training on how to operate, what strategies to devise, how to acquire customers, what promotional and relevant sills will be done by our team to all the selected campus corps.
  • There will be both live and recorder videos on a step-by-step procedures on every topic. All the content can be accesed once you become a campus corp.
